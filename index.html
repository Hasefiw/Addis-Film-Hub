About Us

Addis Film Hub is a creative space dedicated to nurturing the next generation of Ethiopian filmmakers. Our mission is to provide a platform where storytellers can learn, grow, and thrive. We offer:

  • State-of-the-art facilities for filming and editing.
  • Workshops and mentorship programs led by industry experts.
  • A vibrant community of filmmakers to collaborate with.
  • Opportunities to showcase your work at local and international events.

Join us in shaping the future of Ethiopian cinema!
